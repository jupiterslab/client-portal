Clarity is not an abstract concept — it is the first revenue decision you make. Before you price, position, or pitch, this stage answers the foundational question: what are you actually building, for whom, and why will they pay for it?

Complete all six diagnostic responses. Your answers should be direct and specific — not aspirational. Describe what is true right now, not what you want it to be.
The 5 Clarity Layers

Before answering the diagnostic questions, read the five clarity layers below. These are the areas we will assess together.

01 Offer Identity — What do you actually sell? Not what you think you sell. The gap between perceived offer and actual transformation is where most revenue leaks happen.
02 Audience Alignment — Who is ready, willing, and able to pay you right now? Not your dream client — your current client.
03 Transformation Mapping — Where is the client before you, and where are they after? The clearer this bridge, the easier the sale.
04 Sequence Audit — Are you building in the right order? Sequence errors almost always trace back to revenue leaks.
05 Revenue Readiness Score — A diagnostic snapshot of how ready your business is to generate consistent revenue.
Diagnostic Questions — Clarify

Answer each question directly. These are the exact questions your consultant uses to assess revenue readiness.

Question 1
Can you describe your offer in one sentence a stranger would understand?
Question 2
Who paid you last, and why did they choose you over anyone else?
Question 3
What does your client's life look like 90 days after working with you?
Question 4
What are you building right now that doesn't directly generate revenue?
Question 5
If revenue doubled tomorrow, what would break first?
Question 6
What's the real reason your last ideal client didn't move forward?

Revenue clarity means knowing exactly what you sell, what it costs, and how it converts — before you scale anything. This stage structures your offer suite and pricing logic, and identifies where money is leaking right now.

Diagnostic Questions — Revenue
Question 1
What is your current offer suite? List every service or product you sell with its price.
Question 2
What do you need to earn monthly at minimum? Work backward — how many clients at what price gets you there?
Question 3
Where are you underpricing? What would you charge if you priced to the outcome, not the hour?
Question 4
What is your current conversion rate from inquiry to paid client?
Question 5
Which of the three revenue leaks applies to you most right now: Offer Confusion, Sequence Error, or Underpricing?
The 3 Revenue Leaks — Assessed
01 Offer Confusion — Clients can't buy what they can't understand. Vague positioning kills conversion before the conversation begins.
02 Sequence Error — Building audience before offer, scaling before proof, or launching before structure — getting the order wrong bleeds revenue at every stage.
03 Underpricing — Charging less doesn't attract more clients. It signals less value. Aligned pricing attracts aligned clients who execute and get results.
